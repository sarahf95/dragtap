Demo Mode

Letters are input by clicking on a letter group button and dragging to the desired letter. Swipe left on the input area in order to backspace and access capital letters by clicking on the caps toggle button by the text entry area. Symbols and letters can be accessed by swiping toward the center of the screen from the side keyboard toggles.

Test Mode

Copy the provided sentences with the keyboard. There will be 10 phrases total for you to type
